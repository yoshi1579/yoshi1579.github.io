Week1 Blog by Yoshinobu Nakada




1. What are your expectations of the class?

I noticed that we will use many tools such as docker, gtest, and so on in this class, so by the end of this semester, I would like to master how to use those tools and also understand why we use them. Also, even though I have already learned object oriented programming at my previous college, some of the concepts are still not clear to me. Thus, throughout this class, I want to fully understand those unclear concepts.

2. What did you do this past week?

I started working on an open project which is a magazine Android app written in Java.

3. What's in your way?

Since I am an international student, I am not really good at writing English, so by writing this blog, hopefully I can improve my writing skill, which is very important to work in software industry like our professor mentioned.

4. What will you do next week?

Next week class gets into swing (if not full swing), and I will probably find myself spending most of my time eking out a schedule and figuring out precisely what my routine will be on campus each day. I will also probably be spending a lot of time studying for the LSAT on the 16th (my dream is to become a CS patent attorney).

5. What's my experience of the class?

This first week has been a very strange one, but enough has happened that I think I'm getting a feel for how the class will normally proceed. Having a quiz on the second day of class was certainly a surprise; I can see that we will not be messing around.
Dr. Downing hasn't called on me yet, but I can tell it's only a matter of time before he gets around to it. Being put on the spot in such a big way, and being asked to surmise things about stuff I may never have seen before or thought about in my life, is certainly somewhat intimidating. I don't think perfectly when under public scrutiny, but I guess nobody does. There's nothing to do about it, though, but wait for my turn and hope I don't make a complete fool of myself.

6. What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?

I'm new to using git, so I found the following webpage extremely useful for explaining many of the basics of using git via terminal: https://github.com/codepath/ios_guides/wiki/Using-Git-with-Terminal
