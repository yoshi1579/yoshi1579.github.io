5. What's my experience of the class?

This first week has been a very strange one, but enough has happened that I think I'm getting a feel for how the class will normally proceed. Having a quiz on the second day of class was certainly a surprise; I can see that we will not be messing around.
Dr. Downing hasn't called on me yet, but I can tell it's only a matter of time before he gets around to it. Being put on the spot in such a big way, and being asked to surmise things about stuff I may never have seen before or thought about in my life, is certainly somewhat intimidating. I don't think perfectly when under public scrutiny, but I guess nobody does. There's nothing to do about it, though, but wait for my turn and hope I don't make a complete fool of myself.
